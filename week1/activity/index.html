Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

Methods
  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream
  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them with a fork.
  2. Heat the butter in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan and stir gently with a spatula.
  4. Add cream and continue to cook until the eggs are softly set.
  5. Serve immediately.

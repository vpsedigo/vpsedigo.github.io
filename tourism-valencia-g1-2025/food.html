Grab a Bite

Neva's Pizza

Luzuriaga St, Valencia, 6215 Negros Oriental

Neva's in Valencia, Negros Oriental, is an Italian and Asian-inspired restaurant known for its affordable, wood-fired pizzas, pasta, and dishes like Vietnamese Pho and Banh Mi. It offers a casual dining experience with both indoor and outdoor seating, and provides delivery and takeout services. The restaurant is described as having a friendly atmosphere and is popular for both its authentic-tasting Italian food and its unique fusion Asian cuisine.

The Good Glaze

Jose Romero Road, National Highway, Valencia, 6215 Negros Oriental

The Good Glaze in Valencia is a bakery and cafe offering a wide variety of affordable snacks, including donuts, breads, burgers, spaghetti, and pizza, along with frappes and coffee. Located on Jose Romero Road, National Highway, this shop is known for its sweet treats, such as the "classic" glazed donut and "Fudge Brownies," and also offers refreshing drinks like melon and coconut frappes.

Valencia Sunday Market

76JV+FQF, Valencia, Negros Oriental

Every Sunday, Valencia Sunday Market transforms the town plaza into a lively food haven. Local farmers and vendors bring fresh, farm-grown produce — like fruits, vegetables, herbs, and spices — while food stalls serve a wide mix of dishes, from Filipino favorites to international cuisines. Expect flavorful coffee, quesadillas, flavoured milk, wraps, tacos, sweet treats, and freshly made snacks as you browse. The smell of grilled food, baked pastries, and brewed coffee fills the air, making it a favorite weekend spot for residents and visitors who want good food, fresh ingredients, and a relaxed Sunday vibe in one place.

Dulfos Kasagingan

Palinpinon Road, Valencia, Negros Oriental, Philippines

Dulfos Kasagingan — A small local snack spot perfect for a quick break or late-day merienda, Dulfos Kasagingan specializes in classic banana treats and fresh buko juice. Here, you’ll find crispy turon (banana spring rolls), sweet pinaypay fritters, and other banana-based snacks made from saba bananas — all deep-fried and coated in caramelized sugar, giving them a warm, crunchy exterior and soft, sweet center. Pair your snack with a cool glass of buko juice to round it off. Whether you’re coming from a market run or a hike around Valencia, Dulfos Kasagingan makes a great stop for a tasty, affordable snack break.

Tierra Cafe

Tierra Alta, Palinpinon Road, Valencia, Negros Oriental

Terraza Café at Tierra Alta offers a calm spot to enjoy food while taking in the hillside views of Valencia. The café is known for its open, breezy dining area that overlooks the landscape, but even the indoor seating gives you a clear view of the mountains and the coast below. The space feels relaxed and welcoming, making it a nice stop for coffee, pastries, or a full meal after exploring the area. Whether you sit inside or outside, the café’s elevated location gives you wide, scenic views that make the experience feel peaceful and refreshing.

Georg Steakhouse

Mango Ranch, Palinpinon Road, Valencia, Negros Oriental

Georg Steakhouse is a cozy spot in Valencia known for its well-seasoned steaks and warm, rustic atmosphere. The place keeps a simple but welcoming setup, making it easy to relax while enjoying hearty plates like ribeye, porterhouse, or their popular sizzling dishes. They also offer sides and comfort food that pair well with their mains, giving visitors a filling and satisfying meal. It’s a good choice if you want a straightforward, flavorful steak experience without feeling too formal or crowded.

Stay and Relax

Tierra Alta

Palinpinon Road, Valencia, Negros Oriental

Tierra Alta in Valencia, Negros Oriental offers a peaceful mountain-side stay with wide views of the sea, hills, and nearby islands. The resort has a Greek-inspired design, an infinity pool, landscaped grounds, and calm open spaces that make it a good place to relax. Guests can choose from comfortable rooms and suites that include Wi-Fi, air-conditioning, and other basic amenities. You can enjoy meals at their café, spend time by the pool, or walk around the property for scenic views, including the lighthouse area. It’s a quiet spot to rest, a romantic place to stay, or a good base for exploring the natural attractions around Valencia.

Chada Valencia

Valencia in Negros Oriental is a quiet and refreshing town known for its cool air and natural scenery. It sits close to Dumaguete, making it easy for visitors to explore. The town is surrounded by forests, springs, and mountain views, giving people a calm break from busy city life. Valencia offers a mix of nature spots, local food places, and cultural areas that show its warm community and peaceful atmosphere.

Places To Eat

Neva's Pizza

Neva's in Valencia, Negros Oriental, is an Italian and Asian-inspired restaurant known for its affordable, wood-fired pizzas, pasta, and dishes like Vietnamese Pho and Banh Mi.

Neva's Pizza
The Good Glaze

The Good Glaze in Valencia is a bakery and cafe offering a wide variety of affordable snacks, including donuts, breads, burgers, spaghetti, and pizza, along with frappes and coffee.

Good Glaze
Valencia Sunday Market

Every Sunday, Valencia Sunday Market transforms the town plaza into a lively food haven.

Valencia Sunday Market

Places to Visit

Mango Ranch

Mango Ranch is a popular spot in Valencia known for its wide open views and relaxing atmosphere.

Mango Ranch
Pulangbato Falls

Pulangbato Falls is known for its unique red rocks, which give the water and the surrounding cliffs a warm, earthy color.

Pulangbato Falls
Filipino-Japanese Amity Memorial Shrine

The Shrine was built in 1997 to commemorate the end of hostilities and honor the soldiers who fought in the battle for the ridge during World War II.

Japanese Shrine
Cata-al World War 2 Museum

The collection is well-kept and shows the personal effort of the Cata-al family, who gathered these pieces over many years. It’s a quiet place but rich in stories, and it helps visitors understand what life was like during the war in the region.

Cata-al WW2 Museum
Sulfur Vents

The sulfur vents are a geothermal attraction featuring natural steam vents, mud pools, and a strong sulfur smell, a visible sign of the volcanic energy beneath Mount Talinis.

Sulfur Vents

Places to Stay

Tierra Alta

Tierra Alta in Valencia, Negros Oriental offers a peaceful mountain-side stay with wide views of the sea, hills, and nearby islands.

Tierra Alta
